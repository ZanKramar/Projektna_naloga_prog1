  7. lbtaylor1984
    Nov 28, 2016
    8
    Ok as a long term fan of the IP and someone who really enjoyed the original i'm really disappointed with this. The singleplayer story wasOk as a long term fan of the IP and someone who really enjoyed the original i'm really disappointed with this. The singleplayer story was excellent, with good map design and gameplay. The character development was... ok for the most part with the interactions between pilot and titan being the highlight for me. One of the better campaigns i've played in quite some time, but only 5 hours of gameplay it is relatively short, which leaves you with multiplayer.

    Graphics are good, they are nice with good detail on most surfaces and some nice effects to boot on some of the weapons, but it lacks any real shader work, which is surprising when the singleplayer looked much better, with more atmosphere, creating the titanfall universe to great effect, disappointing as the multiplayer is all bright and well lit losing all of that atmosphere, meaning it has nothing on other shooters like BF1 or COD:IW but acceptable for a 2016 title neither the less.

    Sound, good overall, having a good sound with weighty titan footsteps to jet boosters of pilots, music is good in the singleplayer campaign but pretty lacking in multiplayer but the commentary throughout each match was entertaining. Weapons have a great sounds and some beafy kick too with shotguns and LMG's feeling badass and satisfying. nothing is really stand outish in this area imho, it plays the part but theres no sounds which are really remember-able.

    Gameplay again is ok, nothing special here with a lot of the time it being entertaining and interesting, partly to do with match types and partly to do with map design. titan vs titan is particularly boring thought as titans are made out of soft cheese spread this time so you'll be corner peeking all match, if you don't you'll be toast in 7 seconds flat. Because of the map design a lot of the time the game doesn't play smoothly in the sense that there is no fludity to the combat it's slowed right down from TF1 and made it too tactical, forget run and gun with super fast action, prepare to be shot in the back by someone hiding in a corner cloaked, it's boring, it's dumb. Respawn missed the opportunity for larger maps and more players, which the singleplayer story showed to be epic and fun. Unfortunately this isn't TF1+ it's an imposter.

    Maps are lacking real strategy with you either hanging back or getting funnelled into a killzone. Some of the them lack any real design with a few street blocks with a handful of directions to go, others are far too open and a sniper fest. Frankly this is the biggest hangup as it's boring after an hour, as there is a small selection and none of them are really fun.

    Netcode, frankly shocking, i've checked everything on my end bearing in mind i have no problems on any other titles (cod,bf1,bf4,overwatch,etc.), the amount of times the killcam is different from what happened beforehand it is truely eyebrow raising, or when you melee someone in the back nothing happens so you try a few more times only for them to appear behind you and kill you, it's insulting, frustrating and quite frankly poor. there is little lag spikes you can see as a jitter or animations stutter for a second, these happen far too often and ruin any fun or fairness you expect from a title. On top of this sometimes there is no weapon sound until after your dead. you'll see the animations but you'll get no audio feedback so unless your looking at your attacker you get gibbed in silence. Online play is broken as far as i'm concerned with many deaths being completely out of your control.

    I did contact Respawn entertainment who have completely ignored me, which from past experience means there is an issue. When I've contacted other companies about there games with issues you get a response to solve that issue, Respawn are playing dumb so it seems to me there is very much an issue and there keeping quiet and hoping the positive reception is outweighing those of us suffering.

    It has been confirmed that there are a few hacks going about, but Respawn is aware and hopefully fixing very soon,

    Conclusion

    If you like singleplayer stories buy it, it's a great story, it's fun, exciting and has some great set pieces.
    However
    If you want it for multiplayer, buy it as it as good as either COD or BF1 or Overwatch. There all polished titles, but with better netcode at this time.

    As someone who loved the original i'm upset, the netcode is iffy at the moment and the hacks are present. this isn't the titanfall 2 people wanted. This was supposed to have been bigger budgeted with better maps, more titans, more players in a match, with everything polished and refined. Making it a true competitor to COD and BF1, however the netcode does require looking at, I will change this review if the patch changes any of my above statements.    … Expand
